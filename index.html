Chef Nadia Elrhoul has spent decades mastering the culinary traditions of Morocco — from the spice-laden souks of Marrakech to the grand feasts of Fez. Her cooking is a living testament to Morocco's rich gastronomic heritage.

Now based in Miami, Chef Nadia brings that same passion and precision to every event she touches. Whether feeding fifty or five hundred, every dish carries the warmth and depth of a lifelong culinary journey.

The Spread

Signature
Buffet Menu

—01

Seven Moroccan Salads

Morocco's iconic table opener — a vibrant spread of seven cold salads including zaalouk, taktouka, roasted pepper, carrot, and beetroot. Always the centerpiece of any great Moroccan buffet.

—02

Bastilla Fassia

The crown jewel of Moroccan cuisine. Crispy warqa pastry layered with spiced chicken, toasted almonds, saffron, and cinnamon sugar. A dish that commands silence at the table.

—03

Royal Couscous

Hand-rolled semolina steamed three times to perfect lightness, served with slow-braised lamb or chicken, seven vegetables, and a rich aromatic broth. The soul of Morocco in a bowl.

—04

Live Tagine Stations

Slow-cooked directly at your buffet in traditional clay tagines — lamb with prunes and almonds, chicken with preserved lemon, and more. Drama, aroma, and flavor in equal measure.

—05

Harira & Mint Tea Ceremony

Every feast begins with Morocco's beloved harira — a rich tomato, lentil and chickpea soup. And every feast ends with our Moroccan mint tea ceremony, a ritual of warmth and welcome.
